About Cindy

The partner behind your foundation.

Cindy Borbon
Founder & CEO

Cindy Borbon started B SunShine, Inc. because she believes every entrepreneur deserves a real partner. Someone warm, clear, and genuinely excited about your success. Not a form, not a portal, but a person who picks up the phone and speaks your language.

With over 20 years of experience in operations and financial management, she takes the complicated and makes it feel simple. She has led multi-million-dollar operations and brings every bit of that experience, accessibly, directly to you.

B SunShine serves everyone ready to build. First-gen entrepreneurs, women and men led businesses, informal operators ready to get official, and established owners ready to scale. Proudly Latina-owned, bilingual, and rooted in Los Angeles.

Co-Founder of La Comadre Network, a Latina-led nonprofit empowering women in 55+ cities across the U.S. She holds a B.S. in Business Administration and an Accounting Certification — credentials she puts to work for every client.
